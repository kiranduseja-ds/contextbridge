# ContextBridge — AI Communication Gap Detector

World's first NLP system that detects the gap between what someone **meant** and what they **wrote** — then predicts exactly how a reader will misunderstand it.

## Live Demo

## The Problem
Every AI tool today rephrases what you write.
None of them check if what you wrote actually matches what you meant.
ContextBridge solves this.

## What it does
- Scores your message 0-100 for miscommunication risk
- Predicts exactly how your reader will misinterpret it
- Rewrites it in your own voice
